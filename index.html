Artem Sevastopolsky

I am a Ph.D. candidate at Skoltech and a deep learning engineer at the Vision, Learning and Telepresence lab at Samsung AI Center Moscow, where I work on computer vision tasks related to human telepresence under the supervision of Prof. Victor Lempitsky.

At Samsung & Skoltech I worked on realistic differentiable rendering of 3D point clouds, human photo-to-texture synthesis, differentiable warping methods for view resynthesis and other projects. Currently, I'm involved in the development of the relightable rendering of 3D human models. Previously, I was more into medical imaging at Lomonosov Moscow State University and in the industry.
